Call & Deliver: A New Service from NHS Tayside Charitable Foundation

At NHS Tayside Charitable Foundation, we understand how important it is to stay connected with loved ones and friends during their hospital stay. That’s why we are excited to introduce our new service, Call and Deliver, designed to bring comfort and happiness to patients at Ninewells Hospital.

What is Call and Deliver?

‘Call and Deliver’ is a convenient service that allows relatives and friends of patients to send gifts or essential items directly to the wards in Ninewells Hospital. Whether it’s a thoughtful gesture to brighten someone’s day or necessary items to make their stay more comfortable, we’re here to help bridge the gap between you and your loved one.

How does it work?

Using our easy process, you can:

  • Browse our Selection: Visit our dedicated Call and Deliver webpage to view a wide range of products available for delivery. From comforting treats to practical essentials, we have something for everyone.
  • Place Your Order: Once you’ve selected the items you wish to send, simply call our hotline. Our friendly team will assist you with your order, ensuring the right items are chosen for your loved one.
  • Make Your Payment: We’ll process your payment over the phone, making it quick and secure.
  • Delivery to the Ward: Our team of dedicated volunteers will then deliver your selected gifts or items to the specific ward where your relative is staying. You can feel confident that your gesture of care will reach them promptly.
Why Choose Call and Deliver?
  • Convenience: If you are unable to visit the hospital, we take care of the entire process for you.
  • Thoughtful Options: Our curated selection ensures you can choose the perfect gift or essential item based on your loved one’s needs.
  • Support Local Services: By using our service, you are helping to support the vital work of NHS Tayside and its charitable foundation.
Get Started Today!

Make a loved one’s hospital stay a little brighter with our Call and Deliver service.

For questions or further assistance, please don’t hesitate to reach out. Thank you for being a part of our community of care!

Orders can be taken over the phone Monday to Friday between 10.00-15.00.

Call us on 01382 633917 to order
