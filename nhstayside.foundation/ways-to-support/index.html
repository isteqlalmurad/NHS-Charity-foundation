Our Volunteer Retail Team

Can you give three hours a week to support our retail operation?

In this space, you will help by providing excellent customer service, developing cash handling, sales processing and merchandising skills, assisting with stock takes and maintaining the day-to-day care of the gift shop to keep it looking tiptop for our customers. Did you know the money generated from our retail space helps support the charity’s work?

Bill Douglas & Sister Ansell

Legacies & Gifts in Wills

From the everyday to the innovative, we support NHS Tayside to empower the communities it serves: patients, families, donors, carers and staff. Together, we help make people’s lives better and longer. We are dedicated to supporting NHS Tayside patients and staff, and improving health and wellbeing for the people of Tayside.

What will my donation be used for?

  • Buying extra items for patients which will make their visit or stay more comfortable.
  • Supporting major projects, such as a new unit, an extension or a facility upgrade.
  • Introducing new pioneering ways of delivering care.
  • Supporting new medical research.
  • Supporting Volunteer Services.
  • Supporting extra educational and training opportunities for staff.
  • Providing additional support to patients in the community.

Support Us While You Shop

Turn your daily shopping into every day magic!

Did you know that whenever you buy anything online – from your weekly shop to your annual holiday – you could be raising free donations for NHS Tayside Charitable Foundation with easyfundraising? There are over 8,000 retailers on board ready to make a donation – including eBay, Argos, John Lewis & Partners, ASOS, Booking.com and M&S – and it won’t cost you a penny extra to help us raise funds.

Our Volunteer Charity Hub

We have a new charity hub which opened in 2024. In this space, we aim to provide a welcoming area to link visitors/supporters to the relevant NHSTCF team members and provide information about the charity, fundraising, donations, funding programmes and promoting our work.

Our Volunteer Fundraising Team

We are looking to grow a team of volunteers to assist us with our programme of fundraising events throughout the year that people can come along and get involved with. The fundraising events can range from bake sales to marathons, cycle events, charity galas and auctions. If you have experience in participating in or organising fundraising events, the charity and the team can benefit from your experience. The funds raised through this team will 100% go to the charity and support the work of NHSTCF

Corporate Offer

Partnering with NHS Tayside Charitable Foundation demonstrates your commitment to supporting and promoting the health and wellbeing of the people of Tayside and help towards achieving our vision: to support people to live better and longer lives in Tayside.

  • Link to Tayside’s only NHS charity
  • Provide direct support to the communities in which your company operates
  • Enhance your brand
  • Reach a new audience
  • Build your CSR as a company
  • Generate positive promotional opportunities
  • Be associated with a well established health charity with a Tayside focus
